About

Phil Weber began his radio career while still in high school, working as a news reporter and air personality at stations in Sacramento and Portland, Oregon. Later, he dabbled in tax consulting, before ultimately becoming a software developer and technical instructor. He is also an accomplished musician, having performed live and in the studio with a variety of bands.

Phil is passionate about educating and informing his listeners, bringing enthusiasm and humor to even complex technical subjects. He has trained with some of the best in the voiceover business to learn how to convey the emotion behind the script. He looks forward to bringing his diverse background to your next project.
